CCS - Sept 5

What time is it?

Write a function that tells time based on the location of hour hand and the minute hand. The location will be given as two angular values. 0 degree representing 12, 90 degree representing 3, 180 degree representing 6, and so on.

Bonus points for the calculation to be as quick as possible.

Clue: it must be equal to or faster than your own brain, and equally elegant in how it goes about it!
